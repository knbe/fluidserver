christoffel symbol

for a n-dimensional space, the christoffel symbol is a n×n×n array of numbers that describes the curvature of a surface.

you calculate the christoffel symbol (that is, the components of the christoffel symbol) from the metric tensor of the surface/manifold. (the metric tensor is what defines the distance between points on the manifold).

the christoffel symbol defines the covariant derivative. this is a special derivative for a curved surface that accounts for the curvature of the surface.
