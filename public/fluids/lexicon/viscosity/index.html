viscosity

¶ physically: viscosity is a measure of how easily a fluid yields to a shear stress.

  • any time you apply a shear to a fluid, the fluid will move. fluids that more more easily are less viscous, and v.v.

¶ think about the fluid as a series of parallel plates. if there is a shear force, the plates move at different rates.
