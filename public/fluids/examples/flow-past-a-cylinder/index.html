Flow past a Cylinder.

Consider a cylinder placed perpendicular to a flow. Assume ˝⬀v˝ is small compared to the speed of sound, so you can ignore compressibility. Assume gravity is negligible.

  • ˝V˝, the velocity of the flow far before it reaches the cylinder (“upstream velocity”).
  • ˝ρ˝, density
  • ˝ν˝, kinematic viscosity

Initially the flow is stationary. The equations that describe the flow:

  ∇⦁⬀v = 0

(incompressibility)

(time indep. NS, since the flow is stationary and ˝𝝏₍t₎⬀v = 0˝).
